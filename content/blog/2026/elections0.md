The Douglas County Election Commission mailed me (the Clerk) a letter stating:

* **July 10:** deadline for SID to have mailed out notice of the date of election to each property owner. Matt's office will handle this.
* **July 24:** deadline for all Board Members and/or new candidates to have filed their
[Candidate Filing Form](https://votedouglascounty-ne.gov/elections/2026/SID/S26FilingForm.pdf)
with the Douglas County Election Commission.
* **Aug 25:** latest the ballots will be mailed to all property owners. Mailed by the Election Commission.
* **Sep 15:** election of 5 trustees.
