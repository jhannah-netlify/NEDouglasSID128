Inquiry: In looking for wastewater solutions for SID 128, locating a cluster septic
system for the neighborhood on the back portion of a couple lots within the SID
has been determined to be a possible and would have no
effluent and therefore no reporting requirements to the State and EPA.  There
is likely already an easement on these properties for the wastewater line.  Do
you think easements may be feasible (pending landowner agreement) to locate a
consolidated SID septic system on the undeveloped portion of these SID resident
lots?

There would also likely need to be the need for an easement on one other SID
property for maintenance access to the system.

See attached image for approximate location of the septic system in the yellow
box.  The yellow line is the approximate location of the existing wastewater
line.

*Owners can contact any Board member or the SID Lawyer for a copy of this image.*

Initial response: I believe this is feasible and warrants further follow up.  Under
this scenario the SID would not be dissolved because it would need to continue
to oversee the cluster septic system.
